The main working principle of the Lottery Network system

Twitter

Lottery Network was established in May of 2023. Its primary goal is for participants to earn high-value prizes by paying a small amount.

The working mechanism of the system

The participant pays the ticket amount for the lottery. Ticket amounts accumulate in the lottery network pool. Prizes are sent to the winners.

Read the rest of the article for a detailed explanation.

The amount paid to the lottery network pool is transferred, to the lottery network participation pool; The wallet address on the participant's eth chain is transferred. The wallet address on the eth chain is the participant's participation information.

Sample information found in the lottery network prize pool

Users accumulating in the pool . The ranks are shuffled before the draw is drawn. Users with invalid joins will be removed

After the draw made among the users accumulated in the pool, the prizes of the winners are sent to the wallet address they participated in. Thus, we provide a safe and open lottery for everyone.
