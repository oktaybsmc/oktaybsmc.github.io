Rules Details

Detailed rule list

1) The participation fee is the lottery amount specified for the lottery.

2) There is no participation limit for the participant.

3) It is not possible to participate before the drawing start date.

4) It is not possible to participate after the drawing end date.

5) There is a minimum and maximum limit specified for the draw.

6) If the minimum participation limit specified for the lottery cannot be reached, the draw date will be extended. If the minimum participation limit cannot be reached after the extended draw date, it will be transferred to another draw with the same value.

7) If the maximum participation limit specified for the lottery is reached, the draw will take place after 24 hours.

8) For our participants who do not use metamask wallets, you will be included in the lottery if you send a payment equal to the amount of the draw to the specified wallet at the specified chain address.

9) Payments made with Metamask are made in eth chain and eth. Never use other chains.

10) The participation of users who send less than the amount specified for the lottery will be deemed invalid.

11) The rewards of the winning users will be sent to the wallet address they participated in.

12) If a user has participated more than once, he can only win 1 prize from the prize pool.

13) The draws are made live on Lottery Network twitch address and lottery network youtube address.
